Gaming has been a big part of my identity. I grew up admiring the top players and content creators, and I dreamed of reaching their level. Four years later, I achieved that dream. Through time, effort, luck, and a series of risks, I was able to stand out as one of the best players in Fortnite. However, at the top, gaming became more of a job than a passion, so, eventually, I decided to move on and explore. I am always trying to learn and grow by meeting new people and pushing myself to go out of my comfort zone. I strongly believe that comfort prevents growth, so every few weeks I will reflect on how I have grown during that time to make sure I am not getting too comfortable. Furthermore, leadership and entrepreneurship have stuck out to me, so I am looking for more opportunities to learn how to apply the ideas in these subjects to reality.
